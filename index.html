Description of the Project

The "Jalyry" project aims to create a comprehensive application for workshops, specifically designed to facilitate customer organization, note-taking, and scheduling. This technological solution seeks to optimize the operational efficiency of workshops, improving the experience for both owners and customers.

Main Functions

Functionality Description Benefits
Customer Management Registration and tracking of customer information (name, contact, service history). Improves customer relations and facilitates the tracking of their needs.
Note Taking Option to record observations during each visit or service. Ensures a detailed history that helps in future interactions.
Schedule Management Integrated calendar for scheduling appointments and reminders. Increases efficiency and reduces the likelihood of appointment conflicts.
Notifications Alerts to remind about appointments and required follow-ups. Improves punctuality and communication with customers.

Project Design

The application architecture is designed to streamline workshop operations through an intuitive navigation flow. Starting from a secure login, users access a central dashboard that integrates customer management, appointment scheduling, technical notes, and automated reporting, ensuring all data is synchronized and easily accessible.
